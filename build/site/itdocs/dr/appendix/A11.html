A11 | Appendix 11 | WIN DR-TEST ONLY STEPS | 15 MIN

Note
If this is a YEARLY DR Test, shutdown these VM’s at DR and export them to the sandbox environment.

DWV001BNA, DWV001DHCP, DWV001FAXB, DWV001LYNC1, DWV001LYNC2, DWV001MAIL1 - HOT DWV001MAIL2, DWV001OFFWEB, DWV001SQL - HOT DWV051LYNCEDGE1, DWV052LYNCEDGE2 - HOT DWV088VMM, DWV088DC1, DWV088DC2, DWV088DC3, DWV088DC4, DWV011V6SEC - HOT

  1. Open up Hyper-V Manager.

  2. Connect to DWS088hv1.

  3. Right click on each VM.

  4. Choose Shutdown.
